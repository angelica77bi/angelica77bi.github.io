### ✨ What it does

- Browse and book scooters online in minutes  
- Transparent pricing with flexible rental durations  
- Free essentials: helmets, charger, phone holder  
- Delivery & pickup across Bali  
- Optional insurance and ongoing support  

---

### 🌴 Why it exists

Getting around Bali isn’t always easy —  
traffic is dense, taxis are expensive, and traditional rentals can be unclear.

Ayook simplifies mobility:

- No negotiation  
- No hidden fees  
- Just book, ride, and explore
